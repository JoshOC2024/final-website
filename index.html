Season of the Beavers!

Its the season of the Beavers this year! So everybody get ready to represent your team this season because we will need everything you got. We love all the support from past seasons and we need all plus some this year.

Looking forward to returning to the field!

We're looking forward to returning back to the field hopefully at some point the fans will be there with us. We have been working really hard this offseason to be our best yet and want to show the fans and our supporters. We appreciate everything you guys have done for us such as showing support and you deserve to rewarded for all the hardships we've endured this year.

How we're going to get there!

We're going to follow all the guidelines to first make sure that our players and staff are safe before we go forward with planning for mass crowd attendance. From there we will make sure that county infection rates are low prior to making plans for fan attendance. Once we get the go ahead that there is low infection rate we will go ahead with creating a plan for a crowd that is social distanced and that follow safety guidelines.
